1. Create a submission request
FEGA Sweden is under development and can currently only handle a limited number of submission requests. If you consider FEGA Sweden to be the right place for your data and want to be added to our waiting list, please fill in the submission request webform. The FEGA Sweden Helpdesk will evaluate your request and get back to you regarding how to proceed.
2. Specify a DAC
After you have received a confirmation to proceed with your submission, the FEGA Sweden Helpdesk will ask you to provide contact information for the members of the Data Access Committee (DAC). The FEGA Sweden Helpdesk will then verify the designation with each named individual.
3. Receive credentials for a submission account at Central EGA
Once the Data Access Committee has been established, FEGA Sweden Helpdesk will request a submission account for you at central EGA. Using the received credentials, you should be able to log in to the EGA Submitter Portal.
4. Register Study, Sample, DAC and Policy
Use the EGA Submitter Portal to register your Study, Samples, Data Access Committee (DAC) and Policy (including a Data Access Agreement).
5. Upload data
For details, see Prepare and upload data.
6. Register Experiments and Runs
Associate each data file with a registered sample and study by linking files to samples in the EGA Submitter Portal. Details of the experimental procedure you followed must be provided.
7. Finish your submission
Group your runs/analysis into datasets and link them to your new or existing Data Access Committee (DAC) and Data Access Agreement (DAA) using the EGA Submitter Portal. Data requests are done at a dataset level, thus files within a dataset must share release conditions.
8. Release the data
Instruct the FEGA Sweden Helpdesk to release your study. All registered studies are automatically placed on hold until the named submission or Data Access Committee (DAC) contact instructs the SDA Helpdesk for the study to be released.
